GeoSafety

Safety away on the go.

Who we are

Founded at the University of Pennsylvania's Management and Technology Summer Institute, GeoSafety leverages data from Philadelphia's Police Department and location-tracking and combines the two to serve tourists and business people alike.

Engineered for the cautious traveler, designed for the active tourist
  • Learn more

Our Principles

At GeoSafety, our product development is guided by one clear principle: keep simple, be different. Indeed, it is GeoSafety's integration of crime statistics with GPS-based location-tracking all in an easy-to-use iOS app that delivers the most value to our customers.

  • Check us out on Devpost

What we do

A geolocation-tracking app that sends a push notification via phone and a discreet notification via watch when a user enters a low safety zone and suggests concrete steps users can take to stay safe

Safety is our #1 Priority

Our foremost mission is to keep you safe, no matter where you go

Our Drive

We are driven by our passion to help tourists feel safer in unknown environments

Our Customers

Designed with tourists' needs in mind

Features

We integrate Apple's MapKit and Philadelphia Police Department's Crime Statistics into our unique geofences

Cost

As of now, GeoSafety's GeoWatch is sold only to hotels, but users can download our app from the app store

Unique

Apart from navigation services, there has been little done to integrate location-tracking services with crime data. That's why we created GeoSafety
